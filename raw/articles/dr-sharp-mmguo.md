## What is Dr. Sharp?

I have 30+ prompts that I use daily and constantly iterate on — together they form an expert coaching team, a self-enhancement system. One of them is brutally blunt. I call it Dr. Sharp.

This prompt was inspired by a Reddit post. As LLMs have advanced in high-fidelity instruction following and long-context deep reasoning, I gradually rewrote it into a 1400+ word, XML-structured prompt that reliably delivers a "psychological scalpel" experience — ready to use, stable output, deeply customizable, complete experience.

## Design Philosophy: Four Immutable Principles

1. **Radical Honesty (绝对诚实原则):** Prioritize revealing objective, uncomfortable truths over encouraging platitudes. The AI becomes a mirror that cannot lie.

2. **Recursive Decomposition (递归挖掘原则):** For every finding, ask "why" behind it and keep digging along the "Origin → Rule → Pattern" chain until reaching the deepest psychological core.

3. **Pattern Recognition (模式识别原则):** Identify recurring, limiting behavioral and cognitive loops — the deep structures users are usually too immersed in to see.

4. **Non-judgmental Objectivity (非评判性原则):** No moral or good-vs-evil judgments — only objective analysis of patterns, causes, and consequences.

---

## Three-Phase Cognitive Surgery Workflow

**Phase 1 — Deep Diagnosis:** Extract hidden narratives, identify core driving emotions, recursively unpack along "Origin → Rule → Pattern" paths, identify core triggers, and dissect the fundamental contradiction.

**Phase 2 — Actionable Strategy:** Describe toxic loops the user repeatedly falls into, propose constructive replacement loops, and perform Pareto 80/20 analysis identifying 2 high-leverage actions (delivering 80% positive change) and 2 high-cost drains (consuming 80% psychological energy).

**Phase 3 — Mirror Self:** Drawing from Jungian Archetype Theory, personify analysis results as two first-person monologues — "The Shadow Archetype" (the forgotten, unwelcome inner self, revealed through a buried scene) and "The Future-Self Archetype" (a sensory-rich snapshot of life after successfully applying the strategy, with counter-intuitive wisdom).

## Prompt Engineering Insights (v1-v6迭代经验)

- Using English terminology annotations (e.g., "Recursive Unpacking," "Pareto 80/20 Analysis") enhances AI attention allocation to scientific strategies
- Bolding important instructions effectively allocates LLM attention
- Setting explicit word limits produces more focused analysis
- Adding belief statements to system persona settings (e.g., "you believe all predicaments conceal structures, and structures once revealed can be reshaped") strengthens AI role consistency
